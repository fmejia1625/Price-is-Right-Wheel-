Suggested sources for free SFX (verify license before use):

1) Freesound.org
   - Large community library. Many assets under Creative Commons (various types). Search for "casino buzzer", "applause short", "crowd cheer", "drum hit".
   - Use the preview, then click "Download" (account required for some files).

2) Zapsplat.com
   - Free account gives access to many short SFX. Good for UI clicks, stings, applause.

3) Pixabay (Audio)
   - Public-domain / free-to-use music and SFX.

4) Orange Free Sounds, FreeSoundEffects.com
   - Smaller collections but often convenient direct downloads.

Suggested search queries:
- "wheel tick", "mechanical tick", "wheel spin", "buzzer bust", "applause short", "crowd cheer short", "drum sting", "button click"

Notes:
- Prefer short MP3/WAV files (50KB–500KB).
- Keep filenames exactly as expected by the app: spin.mp3, bust.mp3, mega.mp3, applause.mp3, tieStart.mp3, tieWin.mp3, bonus.mp3, click.mp3, tick.mp3
- If a source requires attribution, include a short credits file in `audio/CREDITS.txt` (artist, license, link).
